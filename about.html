About Glace Resin Art

Glace Resin Art is a passion project born from a love of creativity, color, and craftsmanship. Each of our resin creations — whether it's a delicate keychain or a bold nameplate — is handcrafted with love and care to reflect your personal style.

We aim to deliver elegant, affordable, and fully customizable resin pieces that are perfect for gifts, decor, or personal use. Quality and uniqueness are the heart of every product we offer.

Thank you for supporting a small Indian handmade business. We look forward to creating something beautiful for you!
